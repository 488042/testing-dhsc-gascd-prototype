About this service

Get adult social care data is a digital insight tool that provides access to a wide range of datasets relevant to adult social care in England.

The service brings together data from the Care Quality Commission, Office for National Statistics, NHS England, Capacity Tracker and other sources. Learn more about the data sources.

When new data is published, the service updates automatically so you can get insights based on the most current information.

You can use this service to:

  • access data and track indicators across a range of topics, using the latest available information
  • explore and visualise data with interactive tools that help you to identify patterns, trends and insights
  • support decision-making and strategic planning by downloading datasets and charts in formats you can integrate into your own analyses

Available data

The service includes data for a range of indicators, covering key topics in adult social care.

These topics include:

  • present demand: identify the current demand for care, including met and unmet needs, service capacity, and factors driving demand.
  • future demand: understand the likely demand for care, including projected needs by care type, with high-level and detailed data.
  • current market: assess the local care market, including provider availability, occupancy levels, service performance and fee rates.

We'll be adding more topics over time. Tell us which topics would be most useful to you - give your feedback.

The availability of detailed data depends on the source datasets. Some indicators provide data at the national (England) level, while others include more granular data at regional or local authority levels.

For more information about the datasets behind these indicators, you can view the detailed guide to the datasets.

Data sources

Get adult social care data uses the following public and private data sources.

Public data sources

  • Adult Social Care Statistics (Department for Health and Social Care)
  • Adult Social Care Outcomes Framwork (ASCOF) (NHS England)
  • Adult Social Care Report (Department for Health and Social Care)
  • Care Quality Commission (CQC) Care Directory
  • Office for National Statistics collection
  • Short and Long Term Return (SALT) (NHS England)

Private data sources

  • Capacity Tracker
  • Skills for Care
  • Client Level Data (CLD)

For a deeper understanding of the source datasets, including how they are compiled and updated,, view the detailed guide to the datasets.

Quick guide

To access indicators, start by selecting Get data.

You can explore indicators by topic:

  • present demand
  • future demand
  • current market

For quick access, save your preferred indicators to your dashboard.

How to save an indicator to your dashboard

Follow these steps:

  1. Select an indicator to view its details
  2. Tick the checkbox for 'Show this indicator on your dashboard'.

To remove the indicator, untick the checkbox.

Contact us

If you need further support with using the Get adult social care data service, email gascd.enquiries@dhsc.gov.uk.
