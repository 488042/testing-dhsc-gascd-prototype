About Get adult social care data

This digital tool gives you access to reliable, up-to-date data relevant to adult social care in England.

Bringing together data from multiple sources, it helps you uncover valuable insights and make informed decisions.

What this service offers

  • Detailed data to help answer your key questions relating to adult social care
  • Interactive tools to explore and analyse data, benchmark performance and identify trends
  • Data-driven insights to help your organisation drive change and plan for the future

Learn more about Get adult social care data

Turn data into insight

Go beyond numbers. With this tool, you can uncover insights such as:

  • trends in demand for different types of care
  • areas where capacity is under pressure or services are over-provided
  • patterns in how services and care needs vary across different areas and indicators

To explore further, browse data by topic.
