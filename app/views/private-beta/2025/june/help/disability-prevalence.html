What this measures
The percentage of the population in England who are considered disabled.
Source
Office for National Statistics Census 2021 Disability dataset (opens in new tab)

Methodology
Disability status from self-reported data from the 2021 Census.
Limitations
The data is based on survey responses, which may be subject to response bias.

These are disability prevalence estimates for Census Day (21 March 2021), not the present day.
Data definitions
'People with disabilities' refers to individuals who report limitations in daily activities due to long-term physical or mental illnesses.
