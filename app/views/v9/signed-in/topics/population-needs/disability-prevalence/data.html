Information on the local care market

Market Position Statement for Suffolk (opens in new tab)

Most LAs in England publish Market Position Statements (MPS) to help care providers understand the local care market.

You can usually find these MPS documents on LA websites.

MPS documents include information on:

  • supply and demand in the local care market
  • forecasts of future supply and demand for adult social care services
  • how the LA will support the local care market to meet demand
  • how the LA will engage with care providers on challenges in the sector, such as funding pressures
