What this measures
The total financial spend on long-term community adult social care as funded by LAs.
Source
Adult Social Care Activity and Finance Report from NHS England (opens in new tab)
Data correct as of
31 October 2024
Update frequency
Yearly (by financial year)
Methodology
The total financial spend on long-term community social care is calculated by summing the financial spend over the previous financial year for the following categories:

  • home care
  • supported living
  • supported housing
  • community direct payments
  • other long-term community care
The data only covers LA funded long-term social care. It does not include NHS funded care or care funded privately by individuals.
