This page lists all the data indicators (or metrics) used in the Get adult social care data service.

Use the links to view details on the data sources, how the data was collected and any limitations to consider when using the data.

Care provision data indicators

  • Adult social care beds per 100,000 adult population

    Sources: Capacity Tracker, Office for National Statistics.
    Daily updates, limitations apply.

  • Adult social care beds per 100,000 adult population - over time

    Sources: Capacity Tracker, Office for National Statistics.
    Daily updates, limitations apply.

  • Number of community social care providers

    Source: Care Quality Commission.
    Monthly updates, limitations apply.

  • Number of people receiving care from a community social care provider

    Source: Capacity Tracker.
    Daily updates, limitations apply.

  • Occupancy level percentages for adult social care beds

    Source: Capacity Tracker.
    Daily updates, limitations apply.

  • People aged 5 and over who provide unpaid care

    Source: Office for National Statistics.
    Updated every 10 years, limitations might apply.

Funding data indicators

  • LA funding for adult social care

    Source: NHS England.
    Yearly updates, limitations might apply.

  • LA funding for long-term community adult social care

    Source: NHS England.
    Yearly updates, limitations might apply.

  • LA funding for short-term and long-term adult social care

    Source: NHS England.
    Yearly updates, limitations might apply.
