What this measures
The percentages of financial spend on adult social care directed to long-term and short-term care as funded by local authorities.
Source
Adult Social Care Activity and Finance Report from NHS England (opens in new tab)
Data correct as of
31 October 2024
Update frequency
Yearly (by financial year)
Methodology
The percentages are calculated by summing the financial spend on long-term and short-term care over the previous financial year and then dividing these figures by the total financial spend on adult social care over the previous financial year.

The data covers only local authority funded long-term social care. It does not include NHS funded care or care funded privately by individuals.

See data source for details.
