{{serviceName}} is a new service providing access to data on adult social care and related population needs in England. It is managed by the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC).

The service displays a range of the most recent data from Capacity Tracker, the CQC, Office for National Statistics, NHS England and other sources.

You can use this service to:

  • find data on topics such as care provision, funding and population needs
  • compare local data to regional and national statistics
  • explore trends over time
  • download charts and data to use in your own analysis and documentation
