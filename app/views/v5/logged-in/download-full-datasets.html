About these datasets

These files provide a snapshot of the full datasets taken on 1 March 2025. They are updated every month.

Each download is provided as a ZIP file containing one or more CSV files.

If you only need a smaller extract, use the interactive tools in this service to find and download the specific data.

Demographic factors datasets

Population age

  • Population aged 65 and over
  • Population aged 85 and over

Population age - 1 March 2025 (ZIP, 10MB)

Economic factors

  • Geographical areas or population in the most deprived category
  • Homes owned outright

Economic factors - 1 March 2025 (ZIP, 10MB)

Household composition

  • Households with 1 person older than 66 years old

Household composition - 1 March 2025 (ZIP, 10MB)

Health and wellbeing

  • Residents with bad or very bed health conditions
  • Disability prevalance
  • Registered people with learning disability per 100,000 population
  • Registered people with autism per 100,000 population
  • Estimated number of people over 65 with dementia per 100,000 population

Health and wellbeing - 1 March 2025 (ZIP, 10MB)

Overall capacity at local authority level datasets

Unpaid care

  • Residents providing 19 hours or less unpaid care a week
  • Residents providing 20 to 49 hours unpaid care a week
  • Residents providing 50 or more hours unpaid care a week

Unpaid care - 1 February 2025 (ZIP, 10MB)

Capacity in care home sector

  • Adult social care beds per 100,000 adult population, by bed type
  • Beds occupied, by bed type

Capacity in care home sector - 1 March 2025 (ZIP, 10MB)

Capacity in domicilliary care sector

  • Domiciliary care providers per 100,000 adult population
  • Service users per 100,000 adult population
  • Workforce hours paid (agency workers included)
  • Workers employed (agency workers included) in domicilliary care workforce per 100,000 adult population

Capacity in domicilliary care sector - 1 March 2025 (ZIP, 10MB)
